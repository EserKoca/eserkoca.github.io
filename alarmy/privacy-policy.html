Privacy Policy

LoudAlarmy ("the App") is developed by Eser Koca. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. All alarm data (times, settings, sounds) is stored locally on your device using Hive database and is never shared.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized ads. You can control ad personalization through the App Tracking Transparency prompt shown on first launch, or in your device Settings under Privacy & Security > Tracking.

For details on how Google handles data, see Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information.

Permissions

  • Notifications: To alert you when an alarm fires.
  • Motion & Accelerometer: For the shake-to-snooze feature.
  • Tracking (iOS): Optional, for personalized ads via AdMob.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • My alarm didn't ring: Make sure the app has notification permissions, Do Not Disturb is off, and battery optimization is disabled for LoudAlarmy.
  • How do I remove ads? Tap the settings icon and select "Remove Ads" to make a one-time purchase.
  • How do I snooze? Shake your device while the alarm is ringing to snooze.
